Who are we?

The Cooperative Transportation Association of Jerusalem was established by Jerusalemites, young and old, who live in the city, love the city, and want to make it a better place in which to live. The idea of founding a cooperative emerged out of the widening gap between the blossoming of the entertainment and cultural activities offered on Shabbat and the lack of accessibility to them. We want to change the current situation in which only people with cars, or with ample means, can enjoy their weekends. We want to change the situation in which young people take to the driving wheel after drinking because they have no alternative. We want to make the cultural events in the city more egalitarian, more accessible and safer. We want to create a community that travels together and make private transportation inclusive, cooperative and shared.

Why a cooperative?

The Cooperative Transportation Association of Jerusalem assists us in traveling on weekends. The cooperative operates transportation that is private, for members only, on a cooperative basis. Together, we can serve the needs of our community. The more members that join the cooperative, the more we can lower costs, add routes, and increase the frequency of the transportation.

Route and Timetable

Shabus takes you around the city on Friday nights! Dinner with friends? Going for a beer? Off to a party? Take Shabus!

The routes run from 18:00 to 04:00.

Full Schedule

Shabus's main route runs from Pisgat Ze'ev to the City Center, yes Planet, Gilo, Kiryat Yovel, Beit Hakerem and the City Center again, and likewise on the return.

Additional routes connect Mevasseret Zion and Ma'ale Adumim to Jerusalem.

  • Ma'ale Adumim: Schedule (in Hebrew), route map
  • Mevasseret Zion: Schedule (in Hebrew), route map

The routes and frequency may change depending on the decisions of the members of the cooperative. If you want more routes or more frequent rides - tell us and join the cooperative!
